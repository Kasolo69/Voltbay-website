Partnership Summary

Volt Bay proposes to install, operate, and maintain CCS2 DC fast charging infrastructure at your property. You provide designated parking bays and grid access; we handle all capital expenditure, installation, maintenance, and customer support.

Revenue Share Structure

  • All sites: 20% monthly revenue share on gross charging revenue
  • Pilot period: No minimum guarantee — revenue share only on actual usage
  • After pilot: Same 20% share, paid monthly with usage report

Revenue share is calculated on gross charging revenue less payment processing fees and VAT. Paid quarterly within 14 days of period end.

Hardware Specification

  • 30kW DC fast charging via CCS2 connector
  • IP54-rated outdoor enclosure
  • Integrated 4G connectivity and OCPP back-end
  • Dynamic load management to protect building supply
  • LED status indication and RFID/card payment terminal

Service Commitments

  • 48-hour fault response commitment (business hours)
  • Fault reporting via email and WhatsApp
  • Automatic firmware updates
  • Driver support via WhatsApp and email

Term & Exit

Initial term: 90-day pilot with no lock-in. After pilot, rolling monthly agreement with 30 days' notice. Equipment remains Volt Bay property.

Market reality disclaimer: South Africa's EV market is in early-stage growth. As of mid-2026, approximately 4,500 EVs are registered nationally. Early sites may see 2–8 charging sessions per day. Projections are based on conservative, defensible assumptions and are not guarantees of future performance.
